# Design: Research Translation

## Research Questions

1. What do mature bots optimize first: compact defense, refill speed, road continuity, or controller/source proximity?
2. How do bunker layouts avoid congestion: open diagonals, exits, quadrant attendants, road skeletons, or traffic libraries?
3. How do existing-room migrations stage destructive changes safely?
4. Which ideas fit the local pure planner architecture without importing a broad empire framework?

## Output Shape

Create `research/mature-bunker-layout-survey.md` with:

- source summary;
- license posture;
- adopted concepts;
- rejected/deferred concepts;
- child-task mapping.
