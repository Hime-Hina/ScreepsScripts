# Official Starting Room Rules

Sources:

- https://docs.screeps.com/respawn.html
- https://docs.screeps.com/start-areas.html

Observed on 2026-06-09.

## Facts Relevant To Room Selection

- Initial spawn can be placed in a room with a neutral Controller and at least one energy source.
- Official docs recommend choosing a room with two sources and inside a Start Area.
- Novice Areas only allow players with GCL 3 or lower to start there.
- Respawn Areas allow players with any GCL to place the first spawn there.
- After initial spawn placement, the room enters safe mode for 20,000 game ticks.
- A newly placed spawn starts with 300 energy and receives 1 energy per tick until the room's spawn/extension energy reaches 300.

## Impact For This Account

- `docs/game-state.md` records GCL `6`, so Novice Areas are not eligible for this account under current official rules.
- Respawn Areas remain eligible if visible on `shard3`.
- A strong candidate should have two sources unless live evidence shows a better trade-off.
